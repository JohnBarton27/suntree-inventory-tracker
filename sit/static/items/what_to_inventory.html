 No, Do Not Inventory:
  • Furniture (tables, desks, chairs)
  • Cabinets (but not the contents of cabinets)
  • TVs and other large electronics
  • Audio/Video Equipment
  • Items that will eventually need to be replaced due to wear & tear (whiteboards, etc.)
  • Items worth more than $100
  • Leased Equipment (ABS-owned printers, computers, etc.
    • These should already have an ABS sticker/barcode
  • Contents of cabinets/desks/drawers
  • Tubs of "stuff"
  • Knick-knacks (decorative vases, small crosses on walls, etc.)
  • Cords/cables
  • Permanent fixtures (doors, windows, walls, built-in cabinets, etc.)
  • Items worth less than $100
